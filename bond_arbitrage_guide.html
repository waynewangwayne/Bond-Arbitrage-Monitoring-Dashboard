üéØ Primary-Secondary Market Spread Arbitrage

LONG SPREAD Strategy (Buy Primary, Sell Secondary)
Entry Criteria:
  • Z-score < -1.5 (spread abnormally low)
  • Primary auction yield < Secondary market yield by >3bps
  • Bid-to-cover ratio >2.5 (strong auction demand)
  • Auction tail <2bps
Execution Steps:
  1. PRE-AUCTION (Day -1):
    • Submit competitive bid at estimated clearing yield
    • Simultaneously short similar maturity secondary bond
    • Position size: 2-5% of portfolio per trade
  2. AUCTION DAY:
    • Monitor auction results vs expectations
    • If awarded: Hold position
    • If not awarded: Cover secondary short immediately
  3. POST-AUCTION (Day +1 to +5):
    • Monitor spread convergence
    • Target profit: 2-5bps
    • Stop loss: -3bps adverse move
Risk Management:
  • Maximum position: 10% of portfolio across all primary-secondary trades
  • Time stop: Close position after 5 trading days regardless
  • Correlation limit: No more than 3 similar maturity trades simultaneously
Exit Signals:
‚úÖ Take Profit: Spread returns to historical mean ¬± 0.5 std dev
‚ùå Stop Loss: 3bps adverse move or 2 days before next auction
‚è∞ Time Stop: 5 trading days maximum hold period

‚ö° When-Issued (WI) Trading Strategy

WI Trading Framework
Entry Criteria:
  • WI vs Secondary spread >3bps
  • 2-5 days before auction date
  • Normal market conditions (VIX <25)
  • Sufficient WI liquidity (>$10M daily volume)
SELL WI, BUY SECONDARY (WI trading rich):
Day -3 to -2 Before Auction: 1. Sell WI bonds via primary dealer network 2. Buy comparable secondary market bonds (duration-matched) 3. Target spread: 3-6bps profit potential Position Sizing: - Conservative: 1-2% portfolio per trade - Aggressive: 3-5% portfolio per trade
Platform Selection & Timing:
  • Institutional: Bloomberg TSOX, BrokerTec
  • Best Entry: 2-3 days before auction (maximum uncertainty)
  • Latest Entry: Day before auction (reduced profit potential)
  • Avoid: Day of auction (execution risk too high)
Risk Limits:
‚Ä¢ Single Trade Limit: 5% portfolio maximum
‚Ä¢ Aggregate WI Exposure: 15% portfolio maximum
‚Ä¢ Liquidity Reserve: Maintain 20% cash for unexpected moves

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Pitfalls & How to Avoid Them

Execution Mistakes:
  • Leg Risk: Always execute both sides within 30 seconds
  • Size Mismatch: Verify duration/DV01 matching before trading
  • Settlement Issues: Confirm settlement dates align

  • Ignoring Volatility Regime: Reduce size when VIX >25
  • Correlation Breakdown: Monitor when historical relationships fail
  • Liquidity Misjudgment: Ensure exit liquidity before entry

  • Data Lag: Use multiple data sources for confirmation
  • Technology Failure: Have backup execution methods
  • Position Tracking: Maintain real-time position updates
